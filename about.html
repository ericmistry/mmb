About Us

Hi, I’m Mama, the heart and soul behind Mama's Midnight Bakery. Baking has always been my passion, and I love creating delicious, beautiful treats for my community. Based in Hermantown, Minnesota, I operate under a cottage license, which allows me to use fresh, natural ingredients and bake right from my home. I believe in the power of good food to bring people together, and I’m dedicated to accommodating dietary needs and creating custom orders that make every celebration special.
