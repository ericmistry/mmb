Welcome to Mama's Midnight Bakery

We are a cottage-licensed home bakery based in Hermantown, Minnesota. Our focus is on using clean, natural ingredients and working directly with our customers to accommodate dietary restrictions and create flavors and designs you love. Whether you’re looking for a special treat for an event or a sweet indulgence, we’re here to bake up something delicious just for you!
